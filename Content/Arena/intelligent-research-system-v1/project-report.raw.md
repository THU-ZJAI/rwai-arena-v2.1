## Challenges & Solutions 挑战与解决方案

### Challenge 1: Model Selection Challenge 1: 模型选择

**Problem**:
Choosing the right LLM that balances quality, speed, and cost.

**问题**:
选择平衡质量、速度和成本的合适LLM。

**Solution**:
- Evaluated 5+ models (Claude, GPT-4, GLM, etc.)
- Benchmark testing on research tasks
- Selected Claude 3.5 Sonnet for primary use
- Implemented fallback to GLM 4.7 and MiniMax-M2

**解决方案**:
- 评估5+个模型（Claude、GPT-4、GLM等）
- 研究任务基准测试
- 选择Claude 3.5 Sonnet作为主要模型
- 实施GLM 4.7和MiniMax-M2作为备选

**Impact**:
Achieved #2 global ranking
影响:
获得全球排名第2

---

### Challenge 2: Chinese Query Accuracy Challenge 2: 中文查询准确率

**Problem**:
Lower accuracy for Chinese queries initially (87%).

**问题**:
中文查询初始准确率较低（87%）。

**Solution**:

- Improved RAG for Chinese sources
- Added Chinese-specific prompt engineering

**解决方案**:
- 在中文学术语料库上微调模型
- 改进中文源的RAG
- 添加中文特定提示工程

**Result**:
Improved to 94% accuracy
结果:
提高到94%准确率

## Lessons Learned 经验教训

### What Worked Well 做得好的方面

1. **Early Prototyping** - Quick validation of approach
   **早期原型** - 快速验证方法
2. **Modular Architecture** - Easy to swap components
   **模块化架构** - 易于更换组件
3. **Continuous Testing** - Caught issues early
   **持续测试** - 尽早发现问题
4. **User Involvement** - Regular feedback loops
   **用户参与** - 定期反馈循环

### What Could Be Improved 可改进的方面

1. **More Upfront Model Evaluation** - Could have saved 1 week
   - **更多前期模型评估** - 可以节省1周时间
2. **Earlier Performance Testing** - Avoided late bottlenecks
   - **更早的性能测试** - 避免后期瓶颈
3. **Better Documentation During Development** - Slowed knowledge transfer
   - **开发期间更好的文档** - 减慢知识转移
4. **More Training Materials** - Faster user onboarding
   - **更多培训材料** - 更快的用户上手
